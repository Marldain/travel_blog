Japan Japan's capital is Tokyo.
Its main language is 'Nihongo', with many different dialects depending on region.
Japan's national dish is said to be 'Curry Rice', yet it is argued that 'Sushi' is more noteable due to being its birthplace.
South Korea South Korea's capital is Seoul.
Its main language is 'Hangul', with 6 different dialects based on region.
Koreas main dish is said to be 'Kimchi', a spicy fermented cabbage served as a side for almost every meal.
Nigeria Nigeria's capital is Abuja.
Its main language is 'English', yet there are over 500 different languages depending on country and region.
Nigeria's main dish is without a doubt 'Jollof Rice', which is a spicy rice mixed with tomatoes and herbs for its own unique flavor. Each reagion has its own recipe, and some will fight to the ends of the earth claiming they make the best.
Myanmar Myanmar's capital is Naypyitaw.
Its main language is 'Burmese', with over 100 regional dialects.
Myanmar's national dish is said to be 'Mohinga'. A rice noodle soup with a rich broth made from fish, herbs, and shallots, and supplemented with pith of a banana tree.
Ireland Ireland's capital is Dublin.
Its main language is 'Gaelic', yet English is more commonly practiced.
Ireland's national dish is 'Irish Stew'. A thick and hearty stew made from mutton, potatoes, onions, and other various vegetables. The working mans meal.
